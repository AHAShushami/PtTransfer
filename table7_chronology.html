Table 5 / Figure
Chronology of Malaysia–Thailand Cross-Border Patient Transfer Guideline Development, 2016–2026
2016
Joint Simulation — Kedah & Songkhla
Agreed to use existing guidelines; IHR compliance mandated for infectious disease transfers.
2017
Kelantan-Narathiwat Border Health Sub-Committee
MECC at HRPZ II designated as coordination hub; Sungai-Kolok Hospital as main evacuation zone.
2018
31st Malaysia-Thailand Border Health Goodwill Committee Meeting
Reviewed cross-border land patient transfer proposals; drafted patient transfer guidelines.
2019
32nd Malaysia-Thailand Border Health Goodwill Committee Meeting
Draft SOPs for cross-border patient transfer submitted to both Ministries of Health.
2022
Kedah-Songkhla Border Health Subcommittee Meetings
SOP for patient transfer presented; simulation testing planned.
2023
PHEIC Field Training & Simulation — Bukit Kayu Hitam
Tested agency workflow; recommended ambulance bay improvements and transparent document folders.
2023
33rd Thailand-Malaysia Border Health Goodwill Committee Meeting
Draft SOP for Kedah-Songkhla published in Thailand Country Report.
2023
IEOC THIRA Workshops
Identified 5 major cross-border health threats; proposed sub-national coordination manual.
2024
Songkhla-Kedah Border Health Subcommittee Meeting (January 2024)
Build team for patient transfer process; THIRA Discussion on Rabies.
2024
Kedah-Songkhla Patient Transfer Protocol Preparation Meeting
Finalise the Kedah-Songkhla Patient Transfer Protocol. Delegates from ODPC 12 Songkhla, Songkhla Provincial Health Office, and Perlis State Health Department.
2024
34th Malaysia-Thailand Border Health Scientific Conference
Consensus to adopt Kedah-Songkhla draft as basis for national Malaysia-Thailand guideline.
2026
Four-State Guideline Finalisation Meeting — 21 April 2026
Finalised structure, governance, forms, flowcharts, and operational parameters; targeted for bilateral signing July 2026.
2026
Guidelines Development Meeting — 17 May 2026
Document streamlined for field practicality. Single unified transfer form; CPRC as notification only; emergency pathway outside operational hours; DAMA excluded; PKN approval targeted mid-June 2026; KPK endorsement end July 2026.
Note. BHGCM = Border Health Goodwill Committee Meeting; HRPZ II = Hospital Raja Perempuan Zainab II; IHR = International Health Regulations; KPK = Ketua Pengarah Kesihatan; MECC = Medical Emergency Coordinating Centre; ODPC = Office of Disease Prevention and Control; PKN = Pengarah Kesihatan Negeri; THIRA = Threat and Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment.
